The Dumbbell Lateral Raise

Description

The dumbbell lateral raise is an isolation exercise that stregthens the entire shoulder with an emphasis on the sides of the deltiods muscles.

Instructions

Step 1: Standing in a shoulder-width stance, grab a pair of dumbbells with palms facing inward and let then hang at your sides.

Step 2: Rasie your arms out to the sides until they're at shoulder level.

Step 3: Pause, then lower the weights back to the stating position.
