The Pendlay Row

Description

The pendlay row is an excellent back building compound movement. The pendlay row activates the lats, traps, biceps, and rear delts. The pendlay row is best used to build back thickness, contrary to vertical pulls that build back width.

Instructions

Step 1: Stand with your mid-foot under the bar.

Step 2: Bend over and grab the bar with a pronated grip. Breath in deeply into your diaphragm making sure to keep your knees bent and back neutral and horizontal.

Step 3: Pull the bar against your lower chest.
