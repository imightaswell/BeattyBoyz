The Overhead Cable Tricep Extension

Description

The overhead cable tricep extension is one of the best isolation excercises for tricep hypertrophy. Isolation movements such as the cable tricep extension are best performed at the end of a workout once the compound movements have been done to allow for more energy to be used for compound lifts. Similar to the cable tricep extension the overhead cable tricep extension activates the tricep with specific enfaces on the long head of the tricep.

Instructions

Step 1: Start off standing with your feet shoulder-width apart, keeping your back straight and abs tightly drawn in.

Step 2: Grab and hold onto a rope extension with both hands with your palms facing up and raise the rope over your head as this will be your starting position.

Step 3: Slowly lower the rope attachment in an arc motion behind your head, feeling a stretch and isolating your tricep muscles.

Step 4: Hold this position for a count, then slowly raise the rope back up to the start.
