The Cable Row

Description

The seated cable row is a popular exercise to train the muscles of the upper back, including the lats, traps, and rear deltoids, using a cable stack.

Instructions

Step 1: Sit slightly forward on the platform and grasp the cable attachment. slide hips back, positioning your knees with a slight bend.

Step 2: Pull the handle straight into your chest, bending at the elbow and squeezing your shoulder blades together. Remain upright throughout and do not sway during the movement.
