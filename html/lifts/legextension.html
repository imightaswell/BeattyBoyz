The Seated Leg Extension

Description

Leg extensions are exercise usually done with a level machine. You sit on a padded seat and raise a padded bar with your legs.

Instructions

Step 1: Place your hands on the hand bars.

Step 2: Lift the weight while exhauling until your legs are almost straight and don't loc kyour knees. Keep your back against the backrest and do not arch your back.

Step 3: Exhale and lower the weight back to starting position.
