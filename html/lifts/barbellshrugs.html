The Barbell Shrug

Description

The barbell shrug is an exercise targeting the traps. It's popular in stregth and muscle-focused upper-body training, and often trained on a shoulder day.

Instructions

Step 1: Standinf with your feet shoulder-width apart, place a barbell in front of your thighs, hands shoulder-width apart, palms facing your body, and arms fully extended.

Step 2: Keeping your arms straight, shrug your shoulders as high as you can. Pasue, then lower to start postion.
