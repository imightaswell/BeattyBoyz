The Facepulls

Description

The facepull is a very important but also ignored excercise. Facepulls are an isolation movement that activate the rear delts which are a usually ignored muscle. Since they are an isolation movement it is recommended to do facepulls at the end of a workout to allocate energy to compound lifts.

Instructions

Step 1: Standing with your knees slightly bent and feet shoulder width apart grab the rope
with an underhand grip, with your thumbs up.

Step 2: Take a few steps back from the tower to extend the cable. Keep a strong athletic
stance, activating your core and glutes.

Step 3: Squeeze your shoulder blades together to pull the handles of the rope in toward your
face. Hold the position for 2 seconds.

Step 4: Return to the starting position, making sure to keep your shoulders up.
