The Cable Tricep Extension

Description

The cable tricep extension is one of the best isolation excercises for tricep hypertrophy. Isolation movements such as the cable tricep extension are best performed at the end of a workout once the compound movements have been done to allow for more energy to be used for compound lifts.

Instructions

Step 1: Stand with your feet shoulder width apart and knees slightly bent. Grab the rope attachment on the cable.

Step 2: Imagining your elbow as a pull system breath in and pull the rope down while keeping your elbows in place and shoulders externaly rotated. Squeeze at the bottom of the pull for a full range of motion.

Step 3: Allow the weight to pull the cable back to the starting position in a controlled manner while breating out and keeping your elbows in place.
