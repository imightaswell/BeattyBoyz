The Dumbbell Curl

Description

The dumbbell curl is one of the best isolations movements for bicep activation. Since the bicep curl is an isolation movement it is recommended to perform it at the end of a workout once all compound movements are done to allocate more energy to the compound lifts.

Instructions

Step 1: Hold a dumbbell in each hand and stand with your feet as wide apart as your hips and let your arms hand down at your sides with your palms forward.

Step 2: Pull your abdominals in, stand tall, and keep your knees slightly bent.

Step 3: Curl both arms upward until they're in front of your soulders and slowly lower the dumbbell back down.
